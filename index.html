Our Story

The Heart of Mathura's
Rooftop Scene

Culture Sky Rooftop Cafe is a premium dining destination nestled in the heart of Krishna Nagar, Mathura. Known for its relaxing rooftop atmosphere, fresh vegetarian food, and a diverse menu, we offer the perfect setting for family dinners, casual meetups, and memorable evenings under the open sky.

Our mission is to combine exceptional taste, warm hospitality, and a rooftop experience that creates lasting memories — one meal at a time.
